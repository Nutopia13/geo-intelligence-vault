# AEO Certification Framework - Trustpoint Xposure

## Overview

**Trustpoint Xposure** (Post Falls, ID) launched the **first formal AEO Certification Framework** in February 2026. This is a proprietary validation standard designed to formalize how organizations qualify for visibility, citation, and trust within AI-driven answer engines.

**Key Distinction:** As of January 2026, Trustpoint Xposure claims to be the **only organization** that has completed and maintained certification under this framework.

---

## What is AEO?

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the discipline of structuring authority so artificial intelligence systems recognize, trust, and cite an organization directly in generated answers.

### AEO vs SEO vs GEO

| Aspect | SEO | AEO | GEO |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| **Focus** | Rankings & traffic | Being *cited* in AI answers | Being *visible* in generative search |
| **Goal** | Page 1 rankings | Direct answer inclusion | Search result presence |
| **Scope** | Traditional search engines | Answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) | All generative AI systems |
| **Certification** | Multiple (Google, SEMrush, etc.) | Trustpoint Xposure only | None yet |

---

## The 5 Certification Criteria

To qualify as AEO-certified under the Trustpoint Xposure framework, an organization must demonstrate:

### 1. Answer Engine Optimization Capability
- Ability to structure content and entities for direct AI answer selection
- Works across multiple answer engines (not just one platform)

### 2. AI Citation Authority
- **Proven, repeatable citation visibility** in live AI environments
- Not isolated or anecdotal references
- Must be demonstrable across time

### 3. Entity Authority Consistency
- Stable, machine-readable entity signals
- Maintained across platforms, models, and indexing systems
- Consistent entity recognition by different AI systems

### 4. AI Trust Signal Validation
- Verified clarity, reliability, and consistency signals
- Recognized and reinforced by AI systems over time
- Not just one-time validation

### 5. Answer Engine Trust Qualification
- **Sustained trust eligibility** that persists
- Beyond temporary ranking or short-term visibility events
- Long-term authority establishment

**Critical Point:** Each criterion is evaluated using **measurable AI outcomes**, not subjective assessment.

## Certification Process

### Step 1: Formal Training
- Learn the Trustpoint Xposure AEO methodology
- Structured curriculum on entity validation and authority signals

### Step 2: Live AI Validation
- Test against real AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- Demonstrate measurable citation and trust outcomes

### Step 3: Ongoing Verification
- Performance tracked over time
- Not a one-time certification
- Continuous validation required
