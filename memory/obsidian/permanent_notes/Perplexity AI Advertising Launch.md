## Strategic Implications

### For Advertisers
- New channel: Access to high-intent AI search users
- Contextual relevance: Ads matched to specific queries
- Premium positioning: Appearing as "sources" in responses

### For Content Creators
- Potential revenue stream: Being cited as sponsored source
- Risk: Organic citations may compete with paid placements
- Opportunity: GEO optimization becomes more valuable

### For the Industry
- Signals shift from pure subscription models to hybrid monetization
- Could accelerate ad adoption across other LLM platforms
- Raises questions about disclosure and transparency
